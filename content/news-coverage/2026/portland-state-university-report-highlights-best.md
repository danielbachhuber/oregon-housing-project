A Portland State University study surveyed over 500 people experiencing homelessness in [Portland](/cities/portland), finding that 98% wanted housing and identifying the most effective support services. The "Pathways Study" by PSU's Homelessness Research and Action Collaborative included a 17-person Lived Experience Committee to ensure those with direct experience shaped recommendations for addressing the city's housing crisis. The research aims to guide [Portland](/cities/portland)'s approach to providing housing and services to its homeless population.
