This article contains reader letters responding to previous Willamette Week coverage, with no new housing policy developments. A [Portland](/cities/portland) reader criticizes [Home Forward](/organizations/home-forward) CEO Ivory Mathews' $340,000+ salary while affordable housing applicants struggle, highlighting income inequality in housing administration. Another reader suggests Portland City Council consider a land value tax instead of a vacancy tax to address vacant commercial properties and lots downtown, citing Pennsylvania cities' success with this approach to discourage land speculation and encourage development.
