[Home Forward](/organizations/home-forward) CEO Ivory Mathews spent $7,269 in public funds for a six-day Hawaii trip in October 2024, claiming she attended a four-day captive insurance conference while [Portland](/cities/portland)'s housing authority faced financial challenges and vacant apartment backlogs. The trip was among dozens Mathews took over three years, totaling more than $100,000 in taxpayer-funded travel, including frequent attendance at National Association of Housing and Redevelopment Officials conferences where she successfully campaigned for a leadership position. Home Forward's board of commissioners has defended Mathews' extensive travel despite the agency's struggles to provide affordable housing to low-income residents.
