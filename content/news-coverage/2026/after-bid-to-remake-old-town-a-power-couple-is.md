[Portland](/cities/portland) entrepreneurs Jessie Burke and Jonathan Cohen are selling the Society Hotel and other properties as they relocate to the East Coast, marking their exit from Old Town after years of controversial involvement in neighborhood redevelopment efforts. The couple led the Old Town Community Association until earlier this year and were connected to Made in Old Town, a failed shoe incubator that defaulted on a $7 million Prosper Portland loan in 2025 after using city funds to overpay for buildings. Their departure follows mounting criticism of their approach to neighborhood issues and the collapse of redevelopment projects they championed.
