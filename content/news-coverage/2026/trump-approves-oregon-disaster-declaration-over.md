President Trump approved a federal disaster declaration for Oregon following severe winter storms in December 2025 that caused over $15 million in damage across nine counties, after [Governor Tina Kotek](/people/tina-kotek) requested assistance in February. The declaration allows individuals, nonprofits, and governments in affected counties including Clackamas, Hood River, Lane, Lincoln, Linn, Polk, Tillamook, Union, and Yamhill to access FEMA financial assistance for recovery efforts. The approval comes amid reporting that Trump has rejected disaster aid for Democratic-run states at the highest rate of any president since FEMA's creation, approving only 23% of requests from states with Democratic governors and senators compared to 89% for Republican-led states.
