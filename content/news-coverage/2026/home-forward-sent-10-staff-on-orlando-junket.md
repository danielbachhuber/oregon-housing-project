[Home Forward](/organizations/home-forward) CEO Ivory Mathews took 10 agency staffers to an Orlando conference in September 2024 at a cost of $20,445, where some employees helped her campaign for an unopposed National Association of Housing and Redevelopment Officials board position, according to records obtained by WW. The trip adds to revelations that Mathews spent over $100,000 on agency-funded travel between 2023 and 2025 while [Portland](/cities/portland)'s housing authority struggled with vacant units and financial challenges. Union members representing 205 Home Forward employees expressed "no confidence or trust" in Mathews at a board meeting, though the board has defended her travel as educational.
