The [Portland](/cities/portland) City Council approved a $56 million housing package allocating unspent housing funds to rent assistance, affordable housing loans, eviction prevention, and specific projects including Broadway Corridor and a Hacienda CDC development. The vote ended months of contentious debate among councilors about whether to fund specific projects, with [Loretta Smith](/people/loretta-smith) unsuccessfully advocating for $2.5 million for two Black-led nonprofits and accusing colleagues of "institutional racism" when the proposal was rejected 6-5. Council members Candace Avalos, Jamie Dunphy, Mitch Green, and Angelita Morillo championed the final package, which also returns $8.6 million to the general fund amid budget shortfalls.
