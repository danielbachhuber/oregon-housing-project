The [Portland](/cities/portland) City Council heard two days of testimony over appeals challenging the approved demolition and redevelopment of Lloyd Center mall, with 90 people testifying to save the historic mall and ice rink and 50 supporting the redevelopment. The master plan by Urban Renaissance Group proposes replacing the mall with thousands of new homes, retail space, public plazas, and a 4,000-seat music venue, eliminating the ice rink that has existed since 1960. The City Council will vote on the appeals July 8, one month before the mall's scheduled closure on August 8.
