Multnomah County commissioners sent a letter to Gov. [Tina Kotek](/people/tina-kotek) in December alleging that state bureaucratic barriers prevent people with severe mental illness from accessing Medicaid long-term care programs, forcing them into homelessness and costly cycles of crisis care instead. The commissioners claim the Oregon Department of Human Services discriminates by rejecting applications for people with mental illness diagnoses while processing identical applications for seniors and people with other disabilities, violating federal guidance. State agencies have committed to working with the county to address these gaps, proposing a workgroup to streamline enrollment and clarify responsibilities between county, state, and contractors.
