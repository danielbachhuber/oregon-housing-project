Multnomah County law enforcement has opened a criminal investigation into homeless services provider Sunstone Way following a county review that found the nonprofit misspent over $3.6 million in taxpayer dollars, including funds from [Portland](/cities/portland)'s Supportive Housing Services tax. The investigation by the District Attorney and Sheriff's Office follows allegations that Sunstone Way billed the county for unallowable expenses such as entertainment, staff outings, and inflated payroll costs between July 2024 and February 2026. The nonprofit, which operated multiple shelters and pod villages for Multnomah County and [Portland](/cities/portland), is permanently closing its doors after a whistleblower lawsuit exposed the alleged misuse of public funds.
