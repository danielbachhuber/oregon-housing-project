The historic Lloyd Center Mall in [Portland](/cities/portland) is closing after 66 years, with current owners planning to demolish it for a mixed-use development. The mall has been a cultural landmark for Oregonians, featuring an iconic ice rink and hosting various retailers throughout its history, including a recent resurgence of indie shops. The closure marks the end of an era for one of the Pacific Northwest's most recognizable shopping destinations.
