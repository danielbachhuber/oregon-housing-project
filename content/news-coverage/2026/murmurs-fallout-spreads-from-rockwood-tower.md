Multnomah County faces a shelter crisis after severing ties with East County Housing LLC over alleged accounting discrepancies at Rockwood Tower, a former Best Western in Gresham converted to family shelter with a $6.8 million state grant, leading to foreclosure by WaFD Bank and forcing the county to scramble for replacement shelter space. Home Forward, [Portland](/cities/portland)'s housing authority operating 7,000 affordable units, briefed its board on deteriorating conditions including 14% vacancy rates, widespread tenant rent nonpayment, and buildings plagued by drug dealing and security issues amid mounting financial pressures. Legacy Health is seeking a 22% rate increase from Regence BlueCross BlueShield over two years, citing a 47% cost surge since 2021, risking higher out-of-network bills for members if no agreement is reached by March 31.
