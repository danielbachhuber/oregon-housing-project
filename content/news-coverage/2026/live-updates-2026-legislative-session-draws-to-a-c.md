The Oregon Legislature passed [HB 4082](/legislation/2026/hb-4082), allowing cities to expand their [Urban Growth Boundary (UGB)](/key-concepts#urban-growth-boundary) to add affordable housing for older adults, resolving a conflict with another UGB expansion bill for Woodburn. The bill, requested by Governor [Tina Kotek](/people/tina-kotek), passed the House 41-8 and eliminates the typically expensive and lengthy state approval process for UGB expansions when used for senior housing, manufactured homes, and similar developments. Lawmakers also passed Senate Bill 1501 to use $365 million in state bonds to renovate [Portland](/cities/portland)'s Moda Center, with repayment tied to tax revenues from the venue and Trail Blazers.
