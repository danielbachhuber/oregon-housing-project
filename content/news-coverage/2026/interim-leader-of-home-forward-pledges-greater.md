Michael Buonocore has returned to lead [Home Forward](/organizations/home-forward), [Portland](/cities/portland)'s housing authority, as interim executive director for at least nine months following the resignation of former CEO Ivory Mathews amid controversy over excessive travel spending and operational failures. Buonocore, who previously led the agency from 2014 to 2022, pledged increased transparency, scrapped the CEO title in favor of executive director, and announced 10% salary cuts for senior leaders while promising to address high vacancy rates, slow unit turnover times, and security concerns. The board will hire a third-party consultant to investigate the agency's operations and finances, though Mathews received a $171,000 severance package despite union opposition.
