This article is a collection of letters to the editor responding to previous Willamette Week stories, not a housing policy news article. One letter praises [Portland](/cities/portland) city councilors for visiting Vienna to study social housing, suggesting it could inform local efforts and improve upon [Home Forward](/organizations/home-forward)'s current work. The remaining letters discuss electroconvulsive therapy, railroad traffic congestion solutions, and the Thompson Elk fountain restoration, with no housing policy significance.
