The labor union representing 205 [Home Forward](/organizations/home-forward) employees has declared "no confidence or trust in leadership" after revelations that CEO Ivory Mathews spent over $100,000 on travel between 2023 and 2025, including a seven-day Hawaii trip, while earning a $342,000 annual salary. Meanwhile, [Portland](/cities/portland) Mayor [Keith Wilson](/people/keith-wilson) released an $8.5 billion city budget proposal that cuts 145 jobs and makes substantial reductions to police, fire, and parks departments to close a $160 million general fund gap, drawing sharp criticism from public safety unions. Wilson's budget also proposes withholding $30 million typically transferred to Multnomah County for homeless services to fund the city's own homelessness initiatives instead.
