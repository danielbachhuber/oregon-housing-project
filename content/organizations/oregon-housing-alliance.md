The Oregon Housing Alliance is a statewide coalition of nonprofits, housing agencies, faith communities, and advocacy organizations united around increasing access to safe, stable, and affordable homes in Oregon. The Alliance focuses on state budget and policy advocacy, pushing for increased investment in affordable housing construction, preservation, and rental assistance programs.

The Alliance frequently testifies before the Oregon Legislature on housing-related legislation and works to align its member organizations around shared policy priorities each legislative session.
