The Home Builders Association of Metropolitan Portland (HBA) is a trade association representing homebuilders, remodelers, and related businesses in the Portland metro region. The HBA advocates for policies that support residential construction, opposes regulations it views as barriers to housing production, and provides workforce development and industry education programs.

At the Oregon Legislature, the HBA often testifies on land use, permitting, building code, and zoning legislation affecting residential development in the Portland area.
