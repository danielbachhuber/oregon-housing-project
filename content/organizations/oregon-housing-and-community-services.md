# Oregon Housing and Community Services

Oregon Housing and Community Services (OHCS) is the state agency responsible for administering Oregon's affordable housing programs and funding. OHCS finances the construction and preservation of affordable rental housing, provides down payment assistance and homebuyer education, manages the Housing Choice Voucher (Section 8) program, and coordinates federal housing resources for the state.

OHCS plays a central role in implementing legislative housing priorities and frequently provides testimony on housing legislation to inform lawmakers about programmatic and fiscal impacts.
