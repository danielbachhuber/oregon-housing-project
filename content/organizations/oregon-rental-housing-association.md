# Oregon Rental Housing Association

The Oregon Rental Housing Association (ORHA) is a statewide landlord association representing rental property owners and managers across Oregon. ORHA provides education and resources to landlords on Oregon landlord-tenant law, property management practices, and compliance with state and local regulations.

The organization frequently advocates at the state legislature on legislation affecting rental housing providers, including bills related to rent control, eviction procedures, and landlord-tenant relations.
