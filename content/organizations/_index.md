# Organizations

Oregon advocacy organizations, nonprofits, and industry groups involved in housing policy.

## Housing Advocacy & Policy

- [Oregon Housing Alliance](https://oregonhousingalliance.org) — Coalition advocating for affordable housing funding and policy
- [Home For Everyone](https://homeforeveryone.net) — Regional coalition addressing homelessness in the Portland metro area
- [Community Alliance of Tenants](https://communityallianceoftenants.org) — Statewide tenant rights organization
- [Oregon Rental Housing Association](https://oregonrha.com) — Statewide landlord and rental property owner association

## Homebuilders & Real Estate

- [Home Builders Association of Metropolitan Portland](https://www.hbapdx.org) — Homebuilder trade association for the Portland region
- [Oregon Association of Realtors](https://oregonrealtors.org) — Professional association for real estate agents and brokers

## Research & Nonprofits

- [Oregon Housing and Community Services](https://www.oregon.gov/ohcs) — State agency administering housing programs and funding
- [ECONorthwest](https://econw.com) — Economic consulting firm frequently cited in Oregon housing research
- [Sightline Institute](https://www.sightline.org) — Pacific Northwest think tank covering housing and land use reform
