# Oregon's Housing-Related Legislation

| Date | Bill | Summary |
|------|------|---------|
| 2025 | [HB 2138](/legislation/2025/hb-2138) | Streamlines "middle housing" rules, creating easier paths for duplexes, triplexes, and cottage clusters. |
| 2025 | [HB 2258](/legislation/2025/hb-2258) | Requires LCDC to adopt rules for local governments to expedite housing approvals. |
| 2025 | [HB 3031](/legislation/2025/hb-3031) | Creates the Housing Infrastructure Project Fund for affordable housing development. |
| 2025 | [HB 3145](/legislation/2025/hb-3145) | Allocates $25M for factory-produced housing (modular/prefab). |
| 2025 | [SB 684](/legislation/2025/sb-684) | Amends "residential housing" to include mixed-income projects; creates loan fund. |
| 2025 | [SB 6](/legislation/2025/sb-6) | Requires timely approval (45 business days) for housing permits. |
| 2025 | [SB 52](/legislation/2025/sb-52) | Mandates state study on barriers to market-rate housing. |
| 2025 | [SB 974](/legislation/2025/sb-974) | Expedites land use timeline for multi-unit developments to 45 days. |
| 2025 | [HB 3522](/legislation/2025/hb-3522) | Simplifies process for removing squatters to return units to market. |
| 2025 | [HB 2698](/legislation/2025/hb-2698) | Establishes statewide homeownership goal of 65% by 2030. |
| 2025 | [HB 3746](/legislation/2025/hb-3746) | Shortens construction defect claims statute of repose to 7 years. |
| 2025 | [SB 690](/legislation/2025/sb-690) | Delays evictions for families with infants under one year old. |
| 2025 | [SB 973](/legislation/2025/sb-973) | Requires 30 months' notice before ending affordability programs. |
| 2025 | [HB 3054](/legislation/2025/hb-3054) | Caps rent increases in large manufactured housing communities at 6%. |
| 2025 | [HB 3521](/legislation/2025/hb-3521) | Enhances tenant protections regarding security deposits. |
| 2025 | [SB 599](/legislation/2025/sb-599) | Prohibits landlords from inquiring about immigration status. |
| 2025 | [SB 426](/legislation/2025/sb-426) | Makes property owners jointly liable for certain unpaid contractor wages. |
| 2025 | [HB 3589](/legislation/2025/hb-3589) | Allocates $23M for senior housing development and preservation. |
| 2025 | [HB 3644](/legislation/2025/hb-3644) | Establishes statewide shelter program with $204M funding. |
| 2025 | [HB 3792](/legislation/2025/hb-3792) | Increases low-income energy bill assistance funding cap. |
| 2025 | [SB 814](/legislation/2025/sb-814) | Expands rental assistance for young adults (under 25). |
| 2025 | [SB 83](/legislation/2025/sb-83) | Repeals the statewide Wildfire Hazard Map. |
| 2024 | [SB 1537](/legislation/2024/sb-1537) | $258M housing package; created Housing Accountability and Production Office (HAPO). |
| 2024 | [SB 1530](/legislation/2024/sb-1530) | Part of housing package; funding for infrastructure and housing support. |
| 2024 | [HB 4134](/legislation/2024/hb-4134) | Infrastructure grants tied to affordable housing developments. |
| 2023 | [SB 611](/legislation/2023/sb-611) | Stronger rent control cap: lesser of 10% or 7% + CPI; increased relocation assistance. |
| 2023 | [SB 799](/legislation/2023/sb-799) | Extended pay-or-quit notice to 10 days; 60-day pause on evictions with rental assistance proof. |
| 2023 | [HB 2001](/legislation/2023/hb-2001) | Significant eviction reform (10-day notice) and Oregon Housing Needs Analysis (OHNA). |
| 2023 | [HB 2889](/legislation/2023/hb-2889) | Establishes Oregon Housing Needs Analysis (OHNA) system and Housing Production Strategies. |
| 2023 | [HB 2984](/legislation/2023/hb-2984) | Allows conversion of vacant commercial buildings to housing without typical zoning hurdles. |
| 2023 | [HB 5019](/legislation/2023/hb-5019) | Affordable Housing and Emergency Homelessness Response Package. |
| 2022 | [SB 1536](/legislation/2022/sb-1536) | "Right to Cooling" - prohibits landlords from restricting portable cooling devices. |
| 2021 | [SB 8](/legislation/2021/sb-8) | Allows affordable housing on commercial/institutional land without zone changes. |
| 2021 | [SB 458](/legislation/2021/sb-458) | Allows lot divisions for middle housing developments. |
| 2021 | [SB 291](/legislation/2021/sb-291) | Fair chance housing - individualized assessments for criminal records in tenant screening. |
| 2021 | [SB 282](/legislation/2021/sb-282) | COVID-19 renter protections - extended grace period for back rent repayment. |
| 2021 | [HB 2006](/legislation/2021/hb-2006) | Emergency shelter siting - fast-tracks homeless shelter approvals. |
| 2021 | [HB 3115](/legislation/2021/hb-3115) | Requires "objectively reasonable" regulations for homeless camping. |
| 2021 | [HB 3261](/legislation/2021/hb-3261) | Allows hotel/motel conversion to emergency shelters or affordable housing "by right." |
| 2019 | [HB 2001](/legislation/2019/hb-2001) | "Middle Housing" bill; ended exclusive single-family zoning in most cities. |
| 2019 | [HB 2002](/legislation/2019/hb-2002) | Right of first refusal for affordable housing preservation. |
| 2019 | [HB 2003](/legislation/2019/hb-2003) | Requires cities to develop Housing Production Strategies. |
| 2019 | [SB 608](/legislation/2019/sb-608) | Statewide rent control and "just cause" eviction protections. |
| 2018 | [HB 4006](/legislation/2018/hb-4006) | Requires cities with severely rent-burdened populations to adopt reduction strategies. |
| 2018 | [HB 4007](/legislation/2018/hb-4007) | Raised document recording fee from $20 to $60 for housing funding. |
| 2017 | [SB 1051](/legislation/2017/sb-1051) | Fast-track affordable housing approvals; legalized ADUs statewide. |
| 2017 | [HB 4143](/legislation/2017/hb-4143) | Required 90-day notice for rent increases; banned first-year rent hikes. |
| 2016 | [SB 1533](/legislation/2016/sb-1533) | Lifted inclusionary zoning ban; allowed construction excise tax. |
| 2016 | [HB 4079](/legislation/2016/hb-4079) | Pilot program allowing UGB expansion for affordable housing. |
| 2013 | [HB 2639](/legislation/2013/hb-2639) | Banned source-of-income discrimination (Section 8 vouchers); Landlord Guarantee Program. |
| 2009 | [HB 2436](/legislation/2009/hb-2436) | Created document recording fee ($15) for affordable housing funding. |
| 2009 | [SB 291](/legislation/2009/sb-291) | Capped tenant screening fees; required uniform screening criteria. |
| 2007 | [HB 2735](/legislation/2007/hb-2735) | Required 60-90 day notice for rent increases. |
| 2007 | [Manufactured Park Closures](/legislation/2007/manufactured-park-closures) | Required 365 days' notice for mobile home park closures; relocation assistance. |
| 2001 | [Farmworker Housing](/legislation/2001/farmworker-housing) | Established Farmworker Housing Development Account. |
| 1999 | [Inclusionary Zoning Ban](/legislation/1999/inclusionary-zoning-ban) | Prohibited mandatory inclusionary housing (repealed 2016). |
| 1995 | [Homeownership Assistance](/legislation/1995/homeownership-assistance) | Created Home Ownership Assistance Account for lower-income families. |
| 1991 | [HB 2967](/legislation/1991/hb-2967) | Created Housing Development and Guarantee Account; Emergency Housing Account. |
| 1991 | [OHCS Creation](/legislation/1991/ohcs-creation) | Formed Oregon Housing & Community Services Department. |
| 1990 | [Measure 5](/legislation/1990/measure-5) | Constitutional property tax cap; shifted school funding to state. |
| 1989 | [Lender's Tax Credit](/legislation/1989/lenders-tax-credit) | State tax credits for lenders financing low-income housing. |
| 1989 | [Manufactured Park Purchase](/legislation/1989/manufactured-park-purchase) | Program to help mobile home park residents purchase their parks. |
| 1989 | [Park Ombudsman](/legislation/1989/park-ombudsman) | Created Manufactured Dwelling Park Ombudsman. |
| 1987 | [Housing Agency](/legislation/1987/housing-agency) | Created independent Oregon Housing Agency. |
| 1985 | [Rent Control Ban](/legislation/1985/rent-control-ban) | Made rent control preemption permanent (ORS 91.225). |
| 1983 | [Rent Control Preemption](/legislation/1983/rent-control-preemption) | Temporarily preempted local rent control. |
| 1981 | [Needed Housing](/legislation/1981/needed-housing) | Required clear and objective standards for housing development. |
| 1975 | [Goal 10](/legislation/1975/goal-10) | Statewide planning goal mandating "needed housing" in all communities. |
| 1973 | [SB 100](/legislation/1973/sb-100) | Established statewide land use planning and Urban Growth Boundaries (UGB). |
| 1973 | [Landlord-Tenant Act](/legislation/1973/landlord-tenant-act) | Oregon's first comprehensive tenant protection law (ORS Chapter 90). |
