Restricts mortgage interest tax deductions for second homes and phases out deductions for principal residences, directing resulting revenue to an Oregon Housing Opportunity Account.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB3349)

## Overview

Disallows, for purposes of personal income taxation, mortgage interest deduction for residence other than taxpayer's principal residence<b>, unless taxpayer sells residence or actively markets residence for sale</b>. Phases out allowable deduction for interest for principal residence based upon income. Disallows deduction for principal residence above threshold amount. <b> 
<b>Establishes Oregon Housing Opportunity Account.</b> Transfers amount equal to estimated increase in revenue attributable to restrictions on deduction of mortgage interest to 	<i>accounts in Oregon Housing Fund</i>]<b> account</b>. 
Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2019. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
