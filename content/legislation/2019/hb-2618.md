Requires the State Department of Energy to establish a rebate program for residential and non-residential solar electric systems with priority funding for low-income residential customers.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB2618)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Ken Helm, David Brock Smith, Arnie Roblan, Floyd Prozanski

**Co-Sponsors:** John Lively, Pam Marsh, Rob Nosse, Karin Power, Jeff Reardon, Andrea Salinas, Brian Boquist, Michael Dembrow, Lew Frederick, Jeff Golden, James Manning Jr., Sheri Schouten, Marty Wilde


## Overview

Requires State Department of Energy to adopt by rule program for providing rebates for purchase, construction or installation of residential and <b>certain</b> nonresidential solar electric systems and paired solar and storage systems. Sets forth rebate limits under program. 
Establishes Rooftop Solar Incentive Fund. Continuously appropriates moneys in fund to department to issue rebates. Limits total amount of rebates issued annually for nonresidential systems. Requires certain amount of rebates issued annually to be for systems installed for low or moderate income residential customers and low-income service providers. 
<i>Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department for deposit in Rooftop Solar Incentive Fund.</i>] 
Requires department to submit annual report on program to Legislative Assembly. 
Sunsets January 2, 2024. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
