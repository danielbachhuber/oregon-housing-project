Creates an income tax credit for the operation costs of housing for agricultural workers, extending existing sunset provisions.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/SB114)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Peter Courtney, Chuck Thomsen


## Overview

Creates income tax credit for operation costs of housing for agricultural workers. Provides for refundability of credit and for transferability of credit earned by tax-exempt entity. Imposes limitation on total credits allowed to all owners of housing per tax year. 
Extends sunset for tax credits for owner or operator of agriculture workforce housing. 
Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2020, and before January 1, 2026. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
