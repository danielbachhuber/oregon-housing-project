Shortens the statute of limitations for tort actions by homeowners and homeowner associations regarding residential construction defects.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB2661)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Karin Power, Mark Meek


## Overview

Shortens period of limitation for tort action by homeowner association or association of unit owners arising out of structure construction defect. 
Shortens period of limitation by homeowner arising out of single-family dwelling construction defect. 
Prohibits homeowner association or association of unit owners from initiating or intervening in litigation or administrative proceeding arising out of construction defect unless approved by majority of lots or units voting at meeting called for purpose. 
Establishes separate procedures regarding response to notices of construction defect given by owner that is homeowner association or association of unit owners.
