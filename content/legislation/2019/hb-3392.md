Sets a limit on property taxes due on homesteads of seniors living on fixed income.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB3392)

## Overview

Sets limit on taxes due on homesteads of seniors living on fixed income equal to total amount of taxes due for property tax year preceding first property tax year for which relief is allowed. Abates taxes in excess of limit. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
