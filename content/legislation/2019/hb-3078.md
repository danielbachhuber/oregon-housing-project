Requires building permits to display the contractor's Construction Contractors Board license number and strengthens inspection and licensing compliance requirements.

## Overview

Requires that building permit must show contractor's Construction Contractors Board license number if contractor is known. 
Requires inspector performing building code inspection as part of building inspection program, or for purpose of enforcing municipal ordinance, to require that persons engaged in regulated building activity present evidence of compliance with applicable licensing, registration or certification requirements. 
Requires that municipality ensure person issued permit for work requiring Construction Contractors Board license is holder of Construction Contractors Board license. 
Requires Director of Department of Consumer and Business Services to inform Construction Contractors Board when director has reason to believe person is engaging, has engaged or plans to engage in violation of laws regarding licensing and endorsement of construction contractors.
