## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Paul Evans

**Co-Sponsors:** David Gomberg, Rob Nosse, Cedric Hayden


## Overview

Directs Department of Veterans' Affairs to develop and implement one or more pilot programs statewide to provide training, job placement services, financial assistance, technical support and business creation assistance to veterans in construction and construction materials industries. 
Requires report to Legislative Assembly regarding status of pilot programs by September 15, 2020. 
Sunsets pilot programs on December 31, 2021. 
Declares emergency, effective July 1, 2019.
