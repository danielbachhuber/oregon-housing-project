**Chief Sponsors:** Herman Baertschiger Jr.


## Overview

Requires local governments to allow single-family dwellings within urban growth boundary of city with population greater than 	<i>10,000</i>]<b> 25,000</b> in residential lots platted and zoned for such uses. 
<b>Applies to dwelling unit applications filed on or after March 1, 2020.</b>
