## Overview

Directs Department of Revenue, in consultation with Housing and Community Services Department, to conduct study of imposition and administration of residential vacancy tax to fund affordable housing, and to report findings to interim legislative committees related to revenue and housing on or before September 15, 2020. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
