[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/SB209)

## Overview

Provides for exemption from ad valorem property taxation of first $10,000 of real market value of homesteads. Requires Department of Revenue to index exemption amount annually. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
