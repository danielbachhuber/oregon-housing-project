Creates a $250,000 floor for maximum allowable real market value in the homestead property tax deferral program and extends the program's sunset.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB2452)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Lynn Findley


## Overview

For purposes of eligibility of property for homestead property tax deferral program, creates floor of $250,000 for maximum allowable real market value of homestead. Directs Department of Revenue to adjust maximum allowable real market value floor annually according to change in Consumer Price Index. 
<b>Allows claim for deferral to be filed late, with payment of fee. Modifies procedure for allowing claims for deferral in excess of maximum allowable for property tax year. 
<b>Extends sunset for deferral program.</b> 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
