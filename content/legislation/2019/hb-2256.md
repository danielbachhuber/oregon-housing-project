## Overview

Creates Oregon Housing Crisis Task Force. Requires task force to develop recommendations addressing housing affordability. Requires task force to submit report to Legislative Assembly by September 15, 2020. 
Sunsets task force on December 31, 2020. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
