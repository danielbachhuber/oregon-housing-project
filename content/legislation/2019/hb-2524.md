Requires long-term care facilities and adult foster homes to provide residents with information about Long-Term Care Ombudsman services.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB2524)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Margaret Doherty


## Overview

Requires long term care facilities, residential facilities and adult foster homes to provide residents with information developed by Long Term Care Ombudsman describing availability and services of Long Term Care Ombudsman. 
<b>Excuses Long Term Care Ombudsman from reporting abuse of person who is 65 years of age or older or who is residing in long term care facility if report would violate federal law.</b> 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
