Requires documents related to tenancy termination, forcible entry, and residential foreclosures to include information about veterans' housing assistance.

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Paul Evans, Marty Wilde

**Co-Sponsors:** David Gomberg, Rob Nosse, Rick Lewis, Unknown Legislator


## Overview

Requires persons who send or serve certain documents related to termination of tenancy, forcible entry or detainer	<i>, overdue loan payments for loans secured by residential real property</i>] and residential foreclosures to include certain information regarding assistance that may be available to veterans of armed forces. 
Directs Department of Veterans' Affairs and Housing and Community Services Department to jointly submit written report on veterans' housing programs to interim House committee related to veterans.
