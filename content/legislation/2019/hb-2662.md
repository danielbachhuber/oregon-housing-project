Requires the Housing and Community Services Department to prioritize tax credits for affordable housing projects serving direct care and human service workers.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB2662)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Alissa Keny-Guyer, Laurie Monnes Anderson

**Co-Sponsors:** Rob Nosse, Karin Power, Shemia Fagan, Sara Gelser Blouin, Janeen Sollman


## Overview

Requires Housing and Community Services Department, in certifying tax credits for affordable housing loans, to give priority to projects that develop affordable housing for sponsoring entities' employees who are direct care workers or human service workers. 
Applies to loans made on or after January 1, 2020. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
