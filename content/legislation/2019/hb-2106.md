**Chief Sponsors:** Brian Clem


## Overview

Allows dog training classes to be conducted in farm buildings existing on January 1, 2019, within counties that adopted marginal lands provisions. 
<b>Allows counties to approve up to five additional one-year extensions of land use permits for residential development. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.</b>
