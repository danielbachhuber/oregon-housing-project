Makes transferees of tax-deferred homesteads liable for outstanding deferred property taxes if they use the homestead for more than 90 days after the owner's death.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB2460)

## Overview

Provides that transferee of <b>tax-deferred</b> homestead is 	<i>not</i>] liable for amounts of outstanding deferred property taxes due on homestead if transferee 	<i>receives no interest in real or personal property from estate</i>]<b> is using homestead more than 90 days following taxpayer's death and is potential recipient of homestead under intestate succession or by devise or received homestead from estate of deceased taxpayer or right to homestead by gift or assignment from insolvent taxpayer</b>. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
