Requires county clerks to notify property owners when instruments affecting their real property title are presented for recordation, with exceptions for documents submitted by financial institutions, insurers, or attorneys.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2026R1/Measures/Overview/SB1552)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** [Fred Girod](/people/fred-girod)


## Overview

	Digest: The Act would make the county clerk let an owner know when the clerk gets a document that affects the title to the owner's real property. The Act would make an exception for documents that are turned in by real estate professionals. (Flesch Readability Score: 66.6). 
	Requires the county clerk to notify the owner of real property when an instrument affecting the title to the property is presented for recordation unless presented by a financial institution, an insurer or an attorney, or an employee or agent of the professional person. 
	Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
