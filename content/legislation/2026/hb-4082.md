Allows cities and Metro to add sites to their urban growth boundary for manufactured dwelling parks or affordable housing for older persons.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2026R1/Measures/Overview/HB4082)

## Overview

	Digest: This Act allows cities to add sites to their UGB for manufactured dwelling parks or housing for older persons. (Flesch Readability Score: 62.8). 
	Adds to a temporary UGB addition program an option for each city or Metro to also add to its urban growth boundary a site for manufactured dwelling parks, or for housing for older persons, that is affordable for households with incomes not more than 120 percent of area median income. 
	Sunsets January 2, 2033. 
	Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
