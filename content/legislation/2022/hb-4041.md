Permits the Building Codes Structures Board and Construction Contractors Board to adopt licensing standards for structural masonry and fireproofing in essential facilities.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2022R1/Measures/Overview/HB4041)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Rick Lewis, Paul Evans

**Co-Sponsors:** Dacia Grayber, Bobby Levy, Mark Meek, Anna Scharf, Gregory Smith, Boomer Wright


## Overview

Permits Building Codes Structures Board to adopt licensing standards for performing structural masonry. Permits Construction Contractors Board to adopt licensing standards for applying or installing fireproofing materials in essential facilities. 
Requires Department of Consumer and Business Services to adopt rules to require adequate fireproofing in all newly constructed essential facilities. Specifies minimum provisions required in department's rules. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
