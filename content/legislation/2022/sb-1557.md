Requires the Housing and Community Services Department to distribute funds to housing authorities for affordable housing preservation rental assistance programs for tenants of publicly supported housing being withdrawn.

## Overview

Requires Housing and Community Services Department to 	<i>administer</i>]<b> distribute moneys to specified housing authorities for</b> affordable housing preservation rental assistance pilot 	<i>program</i>]<b> programs</b> for tenants of housing being withdrawn from publicly supported housing. Increases statutory damages owed to tenant with rental assistance for retaliation against or unlawful ouster of tenant. 	<i>Requires department to contract with specified housing authorities to administer pilot program.</i>]
