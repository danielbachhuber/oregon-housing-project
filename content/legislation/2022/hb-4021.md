Extends the period for taxpayers to discontinue participation in the homestead property tax deferral program upon transfer of ownership.

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Julie Fahey

**Co-Sponsors:** David Gomberg, E. Werner Reschke, Bobby Levy


## Overview

Extends period in which taxpayers, upon transfer of ownership of homestead or moving manufactured structure or floating home out of state, may discontinue participation in homestead property tax deferral program in assessment year. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
