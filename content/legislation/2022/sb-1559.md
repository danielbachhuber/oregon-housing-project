Removes the requirement for personal property tax returns for floating homes unless new property or improvements have been added since the preceding assessment year.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2022R1/Measures/Overview/SB1559)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Chris Gorsek, Lew Frederick, Zach Hudson

**Co-Sponsors:** Maxine Dexter, Karin Power, Ricki Ruiz, Tawna Sanchez


## Overview

Provides that personal property tax returns not required for floating homes unless new property or new improvements have been added to property tax account since preceding assessment year. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
