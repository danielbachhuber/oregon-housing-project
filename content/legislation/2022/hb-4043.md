Creates an income tax credit for purchasing publicly supported housing to preserve it as affordable housing between 2023 and 2029.

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** John Lively, Deb Patterson, Courtney Neron Misslin

**Co-Sponsors:** Julie Fahey, Dacia Grayber, Chris Hoy, Bobby Levy, Pam Marsh, Jeff Reardon


## Overview

Creates credit against income taxes for purchasing publicly supported housing to retain as affordable housing. 
Applies to sales of housing completed in tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2023, and before January 1, 2029.
