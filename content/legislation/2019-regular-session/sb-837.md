Allows changes to real market value for property tax assessment purposes based on subsequent property sales within five years of a Department of Revenue or tribunal correction.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/SB837)

## Overview

Authorizes, during five assessment years following correction of real market value by Department of Revenue or tribunal, change to real market value indicated by subsequent sale of property. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
