## Overview

Establishes Oregon Homeless Youth Task Force. Requires task force to develop recommendations addressing youth facing homelessness. 
Requires task force to submit report to Legislative Assembly by September 15, 2020. 
Sunsets task force on December 31, 2020. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
