Requires separate majority votes from cities and annexed territories for annexation elections involving 100 or more acres.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2019R1/Measures/Overview/HB2109)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Brian Clem


## Overview

Requires that, in election proposing annexation, votes from city and territory to be annexed be counted separately to determine separate majorities if territory to be annexed includes 100 acres or more. Requires that votes from city and territory to be annexed be combined to determine single majority if territory to be annexed includes less than 100 acres.
