## Overview

Limits landlord to single applicant screening charge per<b> 60-day period, per</b> applicant applying to rent multiple dwelling units owned or managed by landlord. 
<i>Requires landlord to refund applicant screening charge if applicant qualifies but is not offered dwelling unit to rent.</i>].
