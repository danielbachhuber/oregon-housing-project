## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Jeff Golden


## Overview

<i>Requires Real Estate Agency to study homeowners associations. Directs agency to submit findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to housing no later than September 15, 2024.</i>] 
<b>Eliminates requirement for unanimous consent of owners of affected lots or units in planned community when method of determining liability for common expenses or right to common profits is changed.</b>
