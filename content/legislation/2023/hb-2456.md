Modifies emergency housing and state homeless assistance programs to provide services to school-aged children experiencing or at risk of homelessness.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB2456)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Lisa Reynolds

**Co-Sponsors:** Mark Gamba


## Overview

Modifies emergency housing assistance program and state homeless assistance program to provide services and assistance to school-aged children experiencing or at risk of experiencing homelessness. 
Changes "unaccompanied homeless youth" to "youth experiencing homelessness" for purposes of certain programs. Modifies eligibility requirements for applicants for grants from homeless youth services enhancement program and host home project program. Makes programs permanent. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
