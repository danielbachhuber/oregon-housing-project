Requires state agencies and local governments to inform residents without access to housing of available federal assistance programs and require enrollment.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB241)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Kim Thatcher


## Overview

Requires state agency or local government, before providing assistance or benefits to residents of this state who do not have regular access to, or face barriers in attaining, shelter or housing, employment, health care, child care, food, clothing or other basic necessities to fully inform resident of assistance or benefits available from funded federal programs and require resident to apply for or enroll in federal program of assistance or benefits. 
Specifies requirements for fully informing residents of federal programs of assistance or benefits.
