Establishes a Joint Task Force on Hospital Discharge Challenges to develop recommendations addressing patient discharge to post-acute care settings.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB1079)

## Overview

<i>Requires Oregon Health Authority to study hospital licensing requirements. Directs authority</i>]<b> Establishes Joint Task Force on Hospital Discharge Challenges to develop recommendations to address challenges faced by hospitals in discharging patients to appropriate post-acute care settings. Specifies membership. Requires Legislative Policy and Research Director to staff task force and to research and provide specified information to task force for consideration. Requires task force to provide recommendations for administrative changes not requiring legislation to Governor and to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to health and human services by November 15, 2023. Requires task force</b> to submit<b> final report with</b> findings<b> and recommendations</b> to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to health<b> and human services</b> not later than September 15, 2024. 
<b>Declares emergency, effective on passage.</b>
