Requires the Housing and Community Services Department to award grants and loans for affordable housing predevelopment costs and establishes the Affordable Housing Predevelopment Loan Fund.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB3482)

## Overview

<i>Requires Department of Land Conservation and Development to study housing. Directs department to submit findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to housing no later than September 15, 2024.</i>] 
<b>Requires Housing and Community Services Department to award grants for predevelopment costs of affordable housing and grant to assist nonprofits receiving grants or loans under Act. Sunsets January 2, 2026. 
<b>Establishes Affordable Housing Predevelopment Loan Fund. Continuously appropriates moneys in fund to department to award loans for predevelopment costs of affordable housing. 
<b>Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department for grants and loans. 
<b>Declares emergency, effective on passage.</b>
