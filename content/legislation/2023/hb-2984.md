Allows conversion of commercial buildings to housing within urban growth boundaries while prohibiting parking minimums and limiting system development charges.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB2984)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Pam Marsh

**Co-Sponsors:** Julie Fahey, Michael Dembrow, Jeff Golden, Tom Andersen


## Overview

Allows conversion of building from commercial use to housing within urban growth boundary under certain conditions. Prohibits, for such conversions, local governments from enforcing parking minimums and limits collection of system development charges.
