Establishes an emergency veterans housing program to award grants for residential housing projects serving low-income service-disabled veterans and their families.

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Paul Evans


## Overview

Establishes emergency veterans housing program within Housing and Community Services Department. Requires department, in coordination with Department of Veterans' Affairs and advisory board, to award grants for project costs of residential housing for persons of lower income who are service-disabled veterans and their families. 
Appropriates moneys from General Fund for program. 
Sunsets January 2, 2028.
