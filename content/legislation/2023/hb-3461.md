## Overview

Authorizes temporary deferral of property taxes on homesteads rebuilt after 2020 wildfires in amount by which taxes exceed 103 percent of property taxes on destroyed homestead for 2020-2021 property tax year. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
