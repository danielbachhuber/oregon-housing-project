Specifies additional circumstances for the Construction Contractors Board to revoke, suspend, or refuse construction contractor licenses and requires contractors to notify the board of status changes.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB228)

## Overview

Specifies additional circumstances under which Construction Contractors Board may revoke, suspend, condition or refuse to issue or reissue license for construction contractor, or impose civil penalty for violation of laws that regulate work of construction contractors in this state. 
Provides board with additional rulemaking powers related to obtaining or maintaining construction contractor license and resolving complaints. 
<b>Requires licensed contractors to notify board within 10 days after change in status if contractor's status changes from exempt to nonexempt or from nonexempt to exempt.</b> 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
