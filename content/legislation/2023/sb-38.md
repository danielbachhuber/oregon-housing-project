SB 38 requires agencies to apply permit rules in effect either when a complete application is received or 60 days after submission if deficiencies are not communicated to the applicant.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB38)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** [Lynn Findley](/people/lynn-findley)


## Overview

	Requires certain agencies to apply rules applicable to certain permit application in effect on date complete permit application is received, or on date 60 days after permit application is received if agency does not notify applicant of deficiencies in application.
