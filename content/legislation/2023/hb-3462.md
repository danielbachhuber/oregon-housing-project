Requires state agencies to provide safe, non-discriminatory temporary housing to displaced individuals during emergencies and to assist those ineligible for federal resources.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB3462)

## Overview

<i>Requires Oregon Department of Emergency Management, during certain emergencies, to prioritize immediate provision of housing to displaced individuals.</i>] 
<b>Provides that, if certain state agencies provide temporary housing to displaced individuals in response to emergency, agencies shall ensure housing is safe and provided in compliance with state and federal laws relating to discrimination. 
Provides that,</b> if federal 	<i>housing</i>] resources are made available to affected community<b> in response to emergency</b>, 	<i>requires department to provide housing</i>] <b>certain state agencies may provide similar or equivalent</b> resources to community members who are ineligible to receive federal resources 	<i>due to immigration status</i>]<b>, subject to availability of funds</b>. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
