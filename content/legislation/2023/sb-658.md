Establishes a pilot program providing funding to school districts to increase access to schools for homeless students and improve their academic achievement.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB658)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Tim Knopp

**Co-Sponsors:** [Dick Anderson](/people/dick-anderson), Suzanne Weber, Bill Hansell


## Overview

Establishes pilot program to provide funding to school districts for purposes of increasing access to schools by homeless students and improving academic achievement of homeless students. Directs Department of Education to award grants to school districts participating in pilot program from Statewide Education Initiatives Account. Sunsets pilot program July 1, 	<i>2026</i>]<b> 2025</b>. 
Directs Department of Education to submit report on homeless students, including pilot program, to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to education. 
<b>Directs Department of Education to develop and implement statewide education plan for homeless students.</b> 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
