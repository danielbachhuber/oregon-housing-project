## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** [Dick Anderson](/people/dick-anderson), Pam Marsh, Bill Hansell

**Co-Sponsors:** Maxine Dexter, Suzanne Weber


## Overview

Requires Oregon Facilities Authority to provide financing for infrastructure and predevelopment costs for moderate income housing. Appropriates moneys from General Fund to the authority for such purposes. 
Sunsets January 2, 2026. 
Declares emergency, effective July 1, 2023.
