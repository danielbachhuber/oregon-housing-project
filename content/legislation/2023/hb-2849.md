Directs the Oregon Department of Emergency Management to identify and provide grants to long-term recovery groups, establishing a dedicated fund for their support.

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Paul Evans, Ed Diehl


## Overview

Directs Oregon Department of Emergency Management to identify long-term recovery groups operating in Oregon and to provide grants for their support. 
Establishes Long-Term Recovery Group Fund. Continuously appropriates moneys in fund to department for support of long-term recovery groups operating in areas experiencing emergency. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
