Requires the Department of Human Services to provide financial support to nonprofits to develop affordable housing for college students.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB2879)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Maxine Dexter


## Overview

Requires 	<i>Housing and Community Services Department to study</i>] <b>Department of Human Services to provide financial support to nonprofits to provide</b> affordable housing for college students. 	<i>Directs department to submit findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to affordable housing not later than September 15, 2024.</i>]<b> Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department for such purposes. 
<b>Sunsets January 2, 2026.</b>
