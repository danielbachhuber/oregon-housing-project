## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Cedric Hayden

**Co-Sponsors:** David Brock Smith, Suzanne Weber


## Overview

Establishes subtraction from personal taxable income for eligible rental payments made by eligible renters. 
Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2024, and before January 1, 2030. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
