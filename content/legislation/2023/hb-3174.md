## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Pam Marsh, [Dick Anderson](/people/dick-anderson)

## Overview

Requires Oregon Department of Administrative Services to provide grants 	<i>to local governments</i>] for regional housing 	<i>coordinators</i>]<b> coordination and to local governments to support development of needed housing</b>. 
Requires department to provide grants to Association of Oregon Counties and League of Oregon Cities for specific purposes relating to planning for housing. 
Requires department to analyze local governments' costs in planning for housing. 
Appropriates moneys to department to award grants and conduct analysis. 
Sunsets January 2, 2034. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
