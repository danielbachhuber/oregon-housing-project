Establishes a grant program for replacing diesel and fossil fuel generators with renewable hydrogen-fueled generators, prioritizing emergency shelters and critical public service facilities.

**Co-Sponsors:** Kate Lieber, Bobby Levy


## Overview

Establishes grant program within State Department of Energy to provide grants to fund replacement of generators that use diesel or fossil fuels with renewable hydrogen-fueled generators. Directs department to prioritize awarding grants for emergency shelters and infrastructure, facilities and buildings related to provision of critical public services, health care and utilities. Appropriates moneys from General Fund to Renewable Hydrogen-Fueled Generator Grant Fund. 
Directs department to develop planning goals for development of hydrogen production and storage and other hydrogen-related infrastructure. Directs department to incorporate planning goals into updated state energy strategy.
