## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Gregory Smith


## Overview

Requires that moneys from corporate income and excise tax kicker be used for funding of school construction projects and infrastructure improvements. Limits availability to certain districts. 
Applies to biennia ending on or after June 30, 2025. 
Takes effect only if House Joint Resolution 17 (2023) is approved by people at next regular general election. Takes effect on effective date of constitutional amendment proposed in House Joint Resolution 17 (2023).
