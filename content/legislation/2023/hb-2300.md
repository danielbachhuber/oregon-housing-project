Authorizes adoption of licensing standards for structural masonry and fireproofing in essential facilities, and requires the Department of Consumer and Business Services to adopt fireproofing rules for newly constructed essential facilities.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB2300)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Rick Lewis, Paul Evans


## Overview

Permits Building Codes Structures Board to adopt licensing standards for performing structural masonry in essential facilities. Permits Construction Contractors Board to adopt licensing standards for applying or installing fireproofing materials in essential facilities. 
Requires Department of Consumer and Business Services to adopt rules to require adequate fireproofing in all newly constructed essential facilities. Specifies minimum provisions required in department's rules. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
