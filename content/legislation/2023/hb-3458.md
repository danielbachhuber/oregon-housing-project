## Overview

Limits issues that may be basis for appeal of land use decisions where local government amends comprehensive plan or land use regulations. 
<i>Requires Land Use Board of Appeals to approve land use regulations not in strict conformance with comprehensive plan if they further plan.</i>] 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
