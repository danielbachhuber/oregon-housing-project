Requires the Housing and Community Services Department to develop a pilot program providing housing stipends to individuals relocating to cities for economic development purposes.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB3210)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Paul Evans


## Overview

Requires Housing and Community Services Department to develop strategic housing stipend pilot program to provide housing stipends to individuals to move to city where their labor is expected to benefit economic development of city. Establishes advisory board to consult with department on development of program. 
Requires department to report to interim committee of Legislative Assembly on program on or before September 15, 2026. 
Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department for program. 
Sunsets program on January 2, 2029.
