Directs the Housing and Community Services Department to establish a grant program to provide housing stability for people experiencing homelessness and housing instability.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB918)

## Overview

<i>Requires Housing and Community Services Department to study housing. Directs department to submit findings to interim committees of Legislative Assembly related to housing not later than September 15, 2024.</i>] 
<b>Directs Housing and Community Services Department to establish Oregon Housing Justice Program to provide grants for housing stability. Sunsets program on January 2, 2026. 
<b>Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department for grants. 
<b>Allows department to provide stipends to people with lived experience of homelessness and housing instability for participation in advisory committees, focus groups and surveys.</b>
