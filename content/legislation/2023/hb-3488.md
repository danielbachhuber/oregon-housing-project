Appropriates General Fund moneys to support homeownership and fair housing programs and declares an emergency effective July 1, 2023.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB3488)

## Overview

<i>Requires Housing and Community Services Department to report to Legislative Assembly on homeownership disparities by September 15 of each even-numbered year. Sunsets January 2, 2035.</i>] 
<i>Requires department to identify and remove barriers to down payment assistance for individuals with individual taxpayer identification numbers and to report to Oregon Housing Stability Council.</i>] 
<i>Requires department to convene and provide training programs for homeownership organizations and to evaluate and to report on effectiveness of organization's programs to council.</i>] 
<i>Requires department to research existing financial literacy materials and to develop new materials and financial literacy pamphlet by September 15, 2025.</i>] 
<i>Requires mortgage bankers and brokers to include financial literacy pamphlet with mortgages. Becomes operative January 1, 2026.</i>] 
<b>Appropriates moneys from General Fund to support homeownership and fair housing. 
<b>Declares emergency, effective July 1, 2023.</b>
