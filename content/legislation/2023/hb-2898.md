Extends the allowance for recreational vehicles on residential lots damaged by natural disasters until December 30, 2030.

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Jami Cate, Unknown Legislator

**Co-Sponsors:** Shelly Boshart Davis, Bobby Levy, David Gomberg, James Hieb


## Overview

Extends time during which recreational vehicle may be sited on lot with single-family dwelling that was damaged by natural disaster. Extends allowance until December 30, 2030, for dwellings destroyed in 2020 wildfires.
