Creates an income tax credit for sales of publicly supported housing that will be retained as affordable housing between 2024 and 2030.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB2653)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Nancy Nathanson, Courtney Neron Misslin, Pam Marsh

**Co-Sponsors:** Maxine Dexter, Julie Fahey, Dacia Grayber, Bobby Levy, John Lively, Khanh Pham, Andrea Valderrama, Wlnsvey Campos, Kayse Jama, Deb Patterson


## Overview

Creates credit against income taxes for selling publicly supported housing to retain as affordable housing. 
Applies to sales of housing completed in tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2024, and before January 1, 2030. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
