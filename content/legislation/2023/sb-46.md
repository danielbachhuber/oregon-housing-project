## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Lynn Findley


## Overview

Requires Housing and Community Services Department to provide sample plans and grants for senior cottages. Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department for grant program. Requires department to report on program to interim committee of Legislative Assembly on or before September 15, 2025. 
Sunsets January 2, 2026. 
Declares emergency, effective July 1, 2023.
