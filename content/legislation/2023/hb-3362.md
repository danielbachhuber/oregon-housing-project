Allows counties to validate units of land that had approvals revoked after sale to innocent purchasers and extends appeals for certain land use approvals.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB3362)

## Overview

Allows county to validate unit of land that had been approved for recognition that was later revoked after sale to innocent purchaser. 
Sunsets January 2, 2025. 
Until April 1, 2024, allows additional appeals to Land Use Board of Appeals challenging certain 	<i>template dwelling</i>]<b> land use</b> approvals <b>based on forgery</b> for noninnocent purchasers.
