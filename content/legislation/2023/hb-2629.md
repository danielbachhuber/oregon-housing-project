Lowers the disability threshold for property tax exemption on veterans' homesteads and personal property from a higher level to 10 percent.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/HB2629)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Bobby Levy, Bill Hansell, James Hieb

**Co-Sponsors:** Anna Scharf, Tim Knopp, Kim Thatcher, Suzanne Weber


## Overview

Lowers disability threshold for property tax exemption for homestead or personal property of veterans to 10 percent. 
Applies to property tax years beginning on or after July 1, 2023. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
