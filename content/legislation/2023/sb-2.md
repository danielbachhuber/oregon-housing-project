The bill creates an Oregon personal income tax subtraction for amounts received from renting out a room in a taxpayer's home for tax years 2024-2029.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB2)

## Overview

Creates Oregon personal income tax subtraction for amounts received for renting out room in taxpayer's home. 
Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2024, and before January 1, 2030. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
