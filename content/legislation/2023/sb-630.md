The bill authorizes lottery bonds to fund a pilot program providing grants for housing development on former landfill sites.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB630)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Mark Meek

**Co-Sponsors:** Janelle Bynum, David Gomberg, James Hieb, Lily Morgan


## Overview

Authorizes issuance of lottery bonds and transfer of net proceeds to Oregon Business Development Department. Directs department to use proceeds to <b>develop pilot program to</b> award grants to developers for development of housing on former landfill sites. <b>Requires grant recipient to indemnify state for claims arising from activities of recipient for which grant was awarded.</b> 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
