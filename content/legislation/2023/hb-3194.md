**Chief Sponsors:** E. Werner Reschke


## Overview

<i>Changes method of determining</i>] <b>Increases maximum dollar amounts used to determine </b>whether addition of real property improvements constitutes "minor construction" for purposes of property tax law. <b>Indexes increased amounts to account for inflation.</b> 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
