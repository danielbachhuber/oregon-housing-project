## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Kim Thatcher, Lily Morgan


## Overview

Creates Eviction Mediation Program within Housing and Community Services Department. Requires parties to eviction to participate in program. Appropriates moneys from General Fund to department for program. 
Amends eviction mediation and settlement procedures. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.	.
