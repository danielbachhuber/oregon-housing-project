Creates an income tax credit for landlords' lost rental income resulting from COVID-19 eviction and rent nonpayment prohibitions.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2023R1/Measures/Overview/SB67)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Lynn Findley, Mark Owens

**Co-Sponsors:** Tim Knopp


## Overview

Creates income tax credit for lost rental income of landlord, forgone due to prohibition on evictions and rent nonpayment actions during COVID-19 emergency period. 
Applies to tax years beginning on or after January 1, 2020, and before January 1, 2022. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
