SB1529 modifies the Oregon Health Authority's air conditioner and filter delivery program and allows the Housing and Community Services Department to set maximum reimbursement rates for landlords participating in the Housing Choice Landlord Guarantee Program.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2024R1/Measures/Overview/SB1529)

<b>Digest: The Act makes changes to the OHA air conditioner and filter program and to the OHCS landlord guarantee program. The Act goes into effect when the Governor signs it. (Flesch Readability Score: 63.7).</b>
