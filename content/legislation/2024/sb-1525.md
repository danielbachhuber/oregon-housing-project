This bill extends reporting deadlines for the State Department of Energy, modifies renewable energy and carbon sequestration programs, and transfers funds to support residential heat pump deployment.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2024R1/Measures/Overview/SB1525)

## Overview

<b>Digest: This Act amends and creates laws related to the State Department of Energy. (Flesch Readability Score: 63.4).</b> 
[<i>Digest: This Act extends dates for a state agency to submit certain reports. The Act extends dates for the agency and others to develop goals for carbon storage. The Act changes parts of a grant program to support renewable energy projects. (Flesch Readability Score: 60.0).</i>] 
Extends the deadlines for certain reports by the State Department of Energy. Extends by one year the deadline for the establishment of nonbinding biological carbon sequestration and storage goals for natural and working lands. Modifies provisions of the Community Renewable Investment Program.<b> Modifies provisions related to standby generation facilities and requires public utilities to report to the Director of the State Department of Energy about certain standby generators. Transfers moneys from the Heat Pump Deployment Fund to the Residential Heat Pump Fund.</b> 
Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
