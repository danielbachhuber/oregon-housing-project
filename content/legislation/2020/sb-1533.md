Amends the calculation of maximum payments from the Rent Guarantee Program Fund to landlords on a per-tenant rather than per-landlord basis.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2020R1/Measures/Overview/SB1533)

## Overview

		<i>Authorizes Housing and Community Services Department to provide grants and technical assistance to organizations assisting tenants in eviction proceedings.</i>] 
		<i>Requires department to report to interim committee of Legislative Assembly on expenditures and services before September 15 of each year.</i>] 
	<b>Amends calculation of maximum amount payable by Housing and Community Services Department to landlords from Rent Guarantee Program Fund from per-landlord basis to per-tenant basis.</b>
