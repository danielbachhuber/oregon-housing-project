# SB 291 - Tenant Screening Reforms

Oregon tightened landlord-tenant law in 2009 by capping application screening fees and requiring more uniform screening criteria. The legislature prohibited abusive lease clauses and extended the required notice period for no-cause evictions in many cases. These incremental reforms improved renters' security in the 2000s, ahead of the more sweeping changes to come in the next decade.
