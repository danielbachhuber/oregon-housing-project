Establishes a fund to provide grants for water, stormwater, and sewer bill payment assistance to low-income residential households.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2025R1/Measures/Overview/HB3527)

## Overview

<b>Digest: Creates a fund to help households with low incomes with water, stormwater and sewer bill payments. (Flesch Readability Score: 63.6).</b> 
[<i>Digest: Creates a fund to help low-income households with water and sewer bill payments. (Flesch Readability Score: 63.4).</i>] 
Establishes the Public Drinking Water<b>, Stormwater</b> and Sewer Ratepayer Assistance Fund. Directs the Housing and Community Services Department to provide grants from moneys in the fund to qualified organizations to distribute water<b>, stormwater</b> and sewer bill payment assistance to low-income residential households. 
Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
