Allows tenants to terminate a lease during the fixed term without penalty upon 30 days' notice if the landlord delivers certain termination notices 90 days or more in advance.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2025R1/Measures/Overview/HB2134)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Tawna Sanchez

**Co-Sponsors:** Bobby Levy, Pam Marsh, Susan McLain, Daniel Nguyen, Wlnsvey Campos, Lew Frederick, Sara Gelser Blouin, Jeff Golden, James Manning Jr., Deb Patterson


## Overview

Digest: This Act lets a tenant end a term lease early if the landlord has given notice ending the lease after the term. (Flesch Readability Score: 72.9). 
Allows tenant to terminate a tenancy during the fixed term without penalty upon 30 days' notice if the landlord delivers certain notices terminating the tenancy in 90 days or more. 
[<i>Declares an emergency, effective on passage.</i>].
