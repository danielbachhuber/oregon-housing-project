## Overview

Digest: This Act allows owners of newer historic houses to build an extra house. (Flesch Readability Score: 63.4). 
Redefines "historic home" to include dwellings built before 1974 for the purpose of serving as an accessory dwelling unit to a home newly constructed on rural residential lands.
