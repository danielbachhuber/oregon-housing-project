## Overview

Digest: The Act would create a property tax credit for the home of a person who is on active military duty. The Act would phase out the current partial exemption for such homes. (Flesch Readability Score: 61.6). 
Provides for a property tax credit against the ad valorem taxes imposed on the homestead of a resident serving on active military duty. Phases out the current partial exemption for such homesteads. 
Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
