Proposes simplifying property tax assessment rules for partial exemptions and tax credits on property.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2025R1/Measures/Overview/SJR2)

## Overview

Digest: The measure would simplify how exempt property is taxed. It takes effect only if the people vote for it. (Flesch Readability Score: 72.5). 
Proposes an amendment to the Oregon Constitution to exclude partial exemptions of the assessed value of property, and tax credits against property taxes on property, from the requirement that the maximum assessed value of the property be redetermined when the property is first granted or disqualified from a partial exemption or property tax credit. 
Refers the proposed amendment to the people for their approval or rejection at the next regular general election.
