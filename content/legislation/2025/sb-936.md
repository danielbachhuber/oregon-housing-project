Makes public health infrastructure facilities an allowed use in any zoning district when a regulatory deadline has been established.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2025R1/Measures/Overview/SB936)

## Overview

Digest: This Act allows water cleaning in any land use zone and limits review. (Flesch Readability Score: 65.7). 
Makes a public health infrastructure facility an allowed use on any zone if the Oregon Health Authority, Department of Environmental Quality or United States Environmental Protection Agency has established a deadline for the development of the facility. Limits criteria that a local government may apply in approving the development. Applies to certain applications already under review. 
Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
