## Overview

Digest: This Act reduces minimum lot sizes for farm and forest lands. (Flesch Readability Score: 72.6). 
Quarters the minimum acreage for farm, forest and rangeland lots or parcels. Allows farm, forest or rangeland lots to be created from land without water rights if the land is subdivided to establish at least four dwellings that will share infrastructure and amenities. 
Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
