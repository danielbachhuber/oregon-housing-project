## Overview

Digest: This Act makes agencies report on impacts of current and future rules on home production. (Flesch Readability Score: 61.8). 
Requires all state agencies to prepare a report within 60 days on the impacts of the agency's current rules on construction of new housing and the agency's land that may be available for housing production. Requires the agencies to submit the report to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly related to housing no later than September 15, 2026. Expands, to all state agencies, the requirement to complete housing impact statements upon proposing rules. 
Takes effect on the 91st day following adjournment sine die.
