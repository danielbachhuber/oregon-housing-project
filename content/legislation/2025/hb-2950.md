Requires the Land Conservation and Development Commission to update the statewide land use planning goal relating to citizen involvement by June 30, 2029.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2025R1/Measures/Overview/HB2950)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Thuy Tran, Susan McLain, Tom Andersen

**Co-Sponsors:** Dacia Grayber, John Lively, Jules Walters, Wlnsvey Campos, Lew Frederick, Khanh Pham


## Overview

Digest: This Act makes LCDC update land use goals on public process and makes DLCD suggest changes to how goals are updated. (Flesch Readability Score: 64.6). 
Requires the Land Conservation and Development Commission to update the statewide land use planning goal relating to "citizen involvement" by rule by [<i>June 30, 2027</i>] <b>June 30, 2029</b>. <b>Requires the advisory committee appointed by the Department of Land Conservation and Development to provide the commission with draft recommendations by June 30, 2027.</b> 
Requires the Department of Land Conservation and Development to make recommendations <b>by December 31, 2026,</b> relating to the goal amendment process to the interim committees of the Legislative Assembly [<i>relating</i>] <b>related</b> to land use. 
<b>Establishes the Public Involvement Goal Update Fund. Appropriates moneys to the department for deposit into the fund. 
<b>Sunsets January 2, 2030.</b> 
Declares an emergency, effective on passage.
