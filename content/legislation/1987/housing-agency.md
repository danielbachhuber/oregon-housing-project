# Oregon Housing Agency Creation

In 1987, the state elevated its housing efforts by abolishing the old Commerce Department and creating an independent Oregon Housing Agency (O.L. 1987, ch. 414). This move strengthened state-level focus on affordable housing finance and policy coordination.
