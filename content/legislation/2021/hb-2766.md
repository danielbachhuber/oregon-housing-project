## Overview

Establishes Task Force on Use of Surplus Public Lands for Housing. Requires task force to report findings and recommendations to interim committee of Legislative Assembly related to housing on or before December 31, 2021. 
Sunsets task force December 31, 2022. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
