Amends the definition of manufactured dwelling parks, requires local approval for park reconstruction after natural disasters, and establishes landlord and tenant rights in these parks.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/HB3219)

## Overview

<b>Amends definition of "manufactured dwelling park" to include parks with certain prefabricated structures.</b> Requires local governments to approve reconstruction of manufactured dwelling parks after 	<i>wildfire</i>]<b> natural disasters</b>. 	<i>Authorizes local governments to rezone areas for manufactured dwelling parks where wildfires have creating housing scarcity.</i>] Allows Director of Department of Consumer and Business Services to exempt those manufactured dwelling parks from certain building codes. 
<i>Sunsets January 2, 2026.</i>] 
<b>Establishes respective rights for landlords and tenants of manufactured dwelling parks following natural disaster. Allows landlords to require park tenants to maintain liability insurance.</b> 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
