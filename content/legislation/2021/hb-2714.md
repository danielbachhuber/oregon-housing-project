Requires local governments to approve reconstruction after the 2020 wildfires and limits building and septic permit requirements for such reconstruction.

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Jack Zika, David Brock Smith

**Co-Sponsors:** Vikki Breese-Iverson, Unknown Legislator, Lily Morgan, Bobby Levy


## Overview

Requires local governments to approve certain reconstruction after 2020 wildfires. Limits requirements for building permits or septic permits for reconstruction. Sunsets January 2, 2030. 
Tolls time that city or county may consider nonconforming use discontinued during emergency that limits resumption of use. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
