Directs the Secretary of State to publish the top five most commonly spoken languages by county and requires rental agreements and manufactured dwelling sales contracts to be available in those languages.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/HB2761)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Ken Helm, Dacia Grayber

**Co-Sponsors:** Khanh Pham, Lew Frederick


## Overview

Directs Secretary of State to publish on secretary's website list of <b>top</b> five most commonly spoken languages in 	<i>each county</i>]<b> Oregon</b>, other than English. Directs secretary to update list at least once every 10 years. 
<b>Upon request,</b> requires <b>provision of </b>rental agreements	<i>, statements of policies, facility rules and regulations</i>] and sales contracts for manufactured dwellings 	<i>to be available</i>] in <b>any of top</b> five most commonly spoken languages<b> in Oregon</b> other than English. <b>Provides exceptions.</b> 
<b>Becomes operative on July 1, 2022.</b>
