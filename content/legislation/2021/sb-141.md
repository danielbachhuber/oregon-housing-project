## Overview

Makes percentage of partial property tax exemption for property of vertical housing development projects proportional to allocation of 	<i>equalized floors</i>] <b>floor area</b> to residential housing or low income residential housing, without rounding up or down. <b>Clarifies terminology used for computations of partial property tax exemption.</b> 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
