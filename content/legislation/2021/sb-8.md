[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/SB8)

## Overview

Requires local governments to allow development of certain affordable housing on lands not zoned for residential uses. Allows establishment of certain affordable housing at increased density. 
Expands availability of attorney fees for applicants developing affordable housing and local governments prevailing at Land Use Board of Appeals or on appeal from board.
