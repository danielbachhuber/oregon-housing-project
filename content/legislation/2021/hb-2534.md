## Overview

Requires homeowners associations and condominium associations to review governing documents and remove discriminatory language<b> or certify the nonexistence of such language</b> on or before December 31, 	<i>2023</i>] <b>2022</b>. 	<i>Provides a cause of action for enforcement. Sunsets January 2, 2026.</i>] 
<b>Makes certain discriminatory language in governing documents void and unenforceable.</b> 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
