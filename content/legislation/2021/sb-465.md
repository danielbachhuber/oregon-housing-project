Requires counties to allow restoration or replacement of nonconforming uses, including residential structures, damaged by wildfires until September 30, 2025.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/SB465)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Fred Girod, Christine Drazan

**Co-Sponsors:** Raquel Moore-Green, David Brock Smith


## Overview

Requires counties to allow commencement of restoration or replacement of nonconforming uses damaged or destroyed by certain wildfires until September 30, 2025. 
Sunsets January 2, 2026.
