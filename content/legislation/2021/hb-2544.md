Authorizes the Department of Human Services to award grants to organizations providing services and host homes for unaccompanied homeless youth.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/HB2544)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Barbara Smith Warner, Christine Drazan, Bill Hansell

**Co-Sponsors:** Unknown Legislator, Courtney Neron Misslin, Jeff Reardon, Sheri Schouten, Marty Wilde, Lew Frederick, Sara Gelser Blouin


## Overview

Authorizes Department of Human Services to award two-year grants to organizations that provide services to unaccompanied homeless youth and to organizations that operate host home projects for unaccompanied homeless youth. 
Sunsets grant programs on July 1, 2023. 
Requires department to submit reports on host home project grant program to interim committees of Legislative Assembly relating to housing, homeless youth services and high school education. 
<i>Appropriates moneys to department for statewide assessment and grants.</i>] 
<b>Increases certain General Fund appropriations to Department of Human Services for purposes of carrying out Act.</b> 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
