## Overview

Aligns timing of extension of affordability restriction for publicly supported housing with timing for sending notices. Allows qualified purchasers to make offer upon delivery of<b>, or delivery deadline for,</b> first notice. <b>Requires owners of certain publicly supported housing to provide notice to tenants before expiration of affordability restrictions.</b>
