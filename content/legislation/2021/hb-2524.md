Limits restrictions by local governments and private associations on using property for child care facilities and allows child care development on lands zoned for exclusive farm use.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/HB2524)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Pam Marsh, Jack Zika, Karin Power


## Overview

Places limits on certain restrictions by local governments, planned communities and condominiums on use of property for child care facilities. Allows development of child care facilities as conditional use on lands zoned for exclusive farm use. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
