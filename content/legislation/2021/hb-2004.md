## Overview

Appropriates moneys from General Fund to 	<i>Housing and Community Services Department Revolving Account for low-barrier emergency shelters and to Oregon Department of Administrative Services for navigation centers</i>]<b> Oregon Department of Administrative Services to distribute to Oregon Community Foundation to provide grants to support emergency shelters</b>. 
<b>Requires Oregon Community Foundation to provide report to interim committee of Legislative Assembly on grants no later than September 15, 2022.</b> 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
