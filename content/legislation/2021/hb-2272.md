Requires Lane County to approve planning applications to make permanent certain temporary recreational vehicle parks and campgrounds providing transitional housing for wildfire victims.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/HB2272)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Brian Clem


## Overview

<i>Directs Department of Land Conservation and Development to study issues relating to use of land and report to appropriate committee or interim committee of Legislative Assembly on or before September 15, 2022.</i>] 
<i>Sunsets December 31, 2022.</i>] 
<b>Requires Lane County to approve planning application to make permanent certain temporary recreational vehicle parks or campgrounds providing transitional housing for wildfire victims. 
Sunsets January 2, 2024. 
Allows local governments to consider National Flood Insurance Program requirements in approving application to alter, restore or replace nonresidential use destroyed by 2020 wildfires. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.</b>
