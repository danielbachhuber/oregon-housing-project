## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Courtney Neron Misslin


## Overview

Requires boards of homeowners associations and condominium associations to provide budget summary 30 days prior to adoption. 
Prohibits new charges not in adopted budget and approved by majority of owners. Applies to new charges on or after date of next adopted budget or January 1, 2023.
