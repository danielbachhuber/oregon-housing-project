## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Courtney Neron Misslin, Zach Hudson

**Co-Sponsors:** Dacia Grayber, Marty Wilde, Sara Gelser Blouin, Chuck Riley


## Overview

Prohibits homeowners associations from requiring application of pesticide on owner's property<b> except as necessary for ecological or public health</b>. Requires association to notify owners<b>, upon the owner's request,</b> prior to pesticide application on owners' property.
