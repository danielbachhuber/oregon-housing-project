Exempts residential housing reconstruction from construction taxes when replacing housing destroyed by fire or emergency events.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/HB2607)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** David Gomberg


## Overview

Exempts from construction taxes residential housing being constructed to replace residential housing destroyed or damaged by fire or other emergency event or situation. Applies to residential housing destroyed or damaged on or after January 1, 2020. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
