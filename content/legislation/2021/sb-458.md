Allows land divisions to separate new middle housing units in cities and prohibits homeowners associations from forbidding such partitions.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/SB458)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Lew Frederick, Tim Knopp

**Co-Sponsors:** Deb Patterson


## Overview

Allows land division to separate dwelling units for new middle housing allowed in cities. <b>Applies to divisions permitted on or after July 1, 2022.</b> 
Prohibits homeowners associations or restrictive covenants from forbidding partition. Delays applicability of limits on private middle housing restrictions.
