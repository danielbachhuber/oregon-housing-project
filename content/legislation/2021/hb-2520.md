Requires the Land Conservation and Development Commission to adopt rules allowing counties to justify exceptions to statewide land use goals for renewable energy facilities.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/HB2520)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Ken Helm, Pam Marsh


## Overview

Requires Land Conservation and Development Commission to 	<i>amend</i>] <b>adopt rules no later than July 1, 2022, by which county may justify exception to</b> statewide land use planning goals 	<i>related to energy conservation to incorporate</i>] <b>for</b> development of renewable energy facilities 	<i>and reduction of greenhouse gas emissions and to match state energy policies</i>]. 
<i>Requires commission to provide report on progress to interim committees of Legislative Assembly no later than September 15, 2022.</i>] 
Appropriates moneys to 	<i>Department of Land Conservation and Development for purpose of administering and implementing Act</i>]<b> commission to adopt rules</b>. 
Declares emergency, effective July 1, 2021.
