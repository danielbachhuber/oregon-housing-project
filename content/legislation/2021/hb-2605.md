Imposes tsunami design standards for buildings and structures, including vertical evacuation requirements.

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** David Gomberg, David Brock Smith

**Co-Sponsors:** Paul Evans


## Overview

Imposes tsunami design standards for certain buildings and other structures. 	<i>Allows local government to impose vertical evacuation standards for new or existing buildings and other structures as part of local tsunami evacuation plan.</i>] 
Requires that sites for new structures be evaluated for vulnerability to seismic geologic hazards if structures are major structures or are designated as having specified tsunami risk for design. 
Requires developer of building designated as having specified tsunami risk for design to request determination, scientific evidence and recommendations from State Department of Geology and Mineral Industries. Requires department to report annually regarding requests, determinations and suggestions.
