The bill allows cities and counties to toll time limits for nonconforming uses and requires them to permit restoration of nonconforming uses damaged by wildfires until September 2025.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/SB405)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Floyd Prozanski


## Overview

Tolls time city or county may consider nonconforming use interrupted or abandoned during emergency that limits resumption of use. 
Until September 30, 2025, requires <b>cities and</b> counties to allow commencement of restoration or replacement of nonconforming uses damaged or destroyed by certain wildfires. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
