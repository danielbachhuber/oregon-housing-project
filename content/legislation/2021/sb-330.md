Creates an income tax credit for landlords who forgive unpaid rent and adjusts federal taxable income deductions for tenants.

[Original Bill Text](https://olis.oregonlegislature.gov/liz/2021R1/Measures/Overview/SB330)

## Sponsors

**Chief Sponsors:** Betsy Johnson


## Overview

Establishes income tax credit for 	<i>forgiveness by landlord of tenant's nonpayment of rent or other charges</i>]<b> amount of unpaid rent forgiven by landlord and certified by Housing and Community Services Department</b>. Subtracts from federal taxable income tenant's discharge of indebtedness income attributable to landlord's forgiveness of 	<i>nonpayment</i>]<b> unpaid rent</b>. 
Takes effect on 91st day following adjournment sine die.
