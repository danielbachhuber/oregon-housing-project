## Overview

Requires Housing and Community Services Department to pay to residential and nonresidential landlords certain uncollected rents due between April 1, 2020, and September 30, 2020. Appropriates moneys for payments. 
Sunsets on January 2, 2022. 
Declares emergency, effective on passage.
