# Residential Landlord and Tenant Act (ORS Chapter 90)

Oregon's first comprehensive tenant protection law, effective October 5, 1973. Established minimum habitability standards, security deposit rules, and basic tenant rights and landlord duties, greatly improving protections for renters statewide.
