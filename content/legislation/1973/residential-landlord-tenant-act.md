# Residential Landlord and Tenant Act

Oregon's first comprehensive tenant protection law (ORS Chapter 90), effective October 5, 1973. Set minimum habitability standards, security deposit rules, and basic tenant rights and landlord duties.

[More Information](https://oregonlawhelp.org/resource/residential-landlord-and-tenant-act)
