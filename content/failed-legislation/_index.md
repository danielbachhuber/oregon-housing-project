# Failed Housing-Related Legislation in Oregon

This section documents housing-related initiatives, bills, and ballot measures in Oregon that did not become law. Understanding what didn't pass is just as important as what didâ€”failed legislation reveals political obstacles, controversial policies, and the limits of what's politically feasible in Oregon's housing policy landscape.

## Why Document Failed Legislation?

- **Learning from defeats**: Failed bills often re-emerge in modified form in future sessions
- **Understanding opposition**: Reveals which interests and arguments successfully blocked reforms
- **Historical context**: Shows the evolution of housing policy debates over time
- **Missed opportunities**: Highlights solutions that could have addressed housing challenges

## Scope

This section includes:
- Bills that died in committee
- Bills defeated on the floor
- Ballot measures rejected by voters
- Withdrawn or substantially gutted legislation
- Initiatives that failed to gather enough signatures
