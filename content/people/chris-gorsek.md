# Chris Gorsek

Chris Gorsek served as an Oregon State Representative from 2015 to 2023, representing District 49 (Troutdale/Wood Village area). He was a key advocate for tenant protections during Oregon's housing reform era.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[HB 4143 (2016)](/legislation/2016/hb-4143)**: Chief sponsor of tenant notice requirements bill
- **[HB 2004 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2004)**: Chief sponsor of rent control bill (did not pass)
- **[SB 608 (2019)](/legislation/2019/sb-608)**: Sponsor of statewide rent control legislation
