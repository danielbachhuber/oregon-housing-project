# Vikki Breese Iverson

Vikki Breese Iverson is a Republican State Representative representing District 59 (Prineville). She assumed office on January 9, 2023, and her current term runs through January 11, 2027.[^1]

Breese Iverson is a 5th generation rancher raised in Prineville, Oregon. She graduated from Crook County High School in 1992 and obtained her undergraduate degree after attending Central Oregon Community College and Oregon State University.[^1][^2]

Her professional experience includes working as a realtor and principal broker. She previously worked for former Oregon State Senator Ted Ferrioli and former Oregon State House Speaker Karen Minnis.[^1][^2]

Breese Iverson served as House Minority Leader from November 2021 to September 2023, succeeding Christine Drazan who stepped down to run for governor.[^2]

### As State Representative
