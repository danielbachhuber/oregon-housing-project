# Peter Courtney

Peter Courtney served as Oregon Senate President from 2003 to 2023, making him the longest-serving Senate President in Oregon history. He represented District 11 (Salem area) from 1999 to 2023.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[SB 608 (2019)](/legislation/2019/sb-608)**: Chief sponsor of statewide rent control legislation
- **[SB 8 (2021)](/legislation/2021/sb-8)**: Chief sponsor of bill allowing affordable housing on commercial, public, and religious land
