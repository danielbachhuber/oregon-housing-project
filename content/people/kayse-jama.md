# Kayse Jama

Kayse Jama is an Oregon State Senator representing District 24 (East Portland/Gresham) since 2021. Before serving in the Legislature, he was the executive director of Unite Oregon. He has been a key advocate for tenant protections and housing equity.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[SB 282 (2021)](/legislation/2021/sb-282)**: Chief sponsor of COVID-19 tenant protections bill
