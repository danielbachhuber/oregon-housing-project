# Tim Knopp

Tim Knopp is an Oregon State Senator representing District 27 (Bend area). A Republican, he has been a key bipartisan partner on several housing bills, demonstrating that housing reform can cross party lines.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[SB 458 (2021)](/legislation/2021/sb-458)**: Chief co-sponsor (with Senator Lew Frederick) of bipartisan middle housing land division bill
- **[HB 4123 (2022)](/legislation/2022/hb-4123)**: Chief co-sponsor of homeless services pilot program
