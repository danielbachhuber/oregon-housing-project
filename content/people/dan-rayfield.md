# Dan Rayfield

Dan Rayfield is an Oregon State Representative serving District 16 (Corvallis), and has served as Speaker of the Oregon House since 2023. He has been a key partner in advancing Governor Kotek's housing agenda.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[HB 3414 (2023)](/legislation/2023/hb-3414)**: Chief sponsor of Governor Kotek's housing production bill (did not pass)
