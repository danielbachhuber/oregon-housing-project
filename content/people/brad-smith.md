# Brad Smith

Brad Smith is the Chief Operating Officer at Meersburg Properties, LLC, an affiliate of Windsor Properties, Ltd., a rental housing provider operating in the [Portland](/cities/portland) metro area.[^1][^2] Based in [Hillsboro](/cities/hillsboro), he has over 25 years of experience in the real estate industry, with expertise in residential and light commercial construction, development, and portfolio management.[^1]

Smith has testified before the Oregon Legislature on housing-related bills, representing the perspective of small and mid-sized rental housing providers in Washington County.

## Background

Smith holds a Bachelor of Science in Housing Studies from Oregon State University.[^1] He oversees daily operations and strategic direction at Meersburg Properties / Windsor Properties, which provides single-family rental homes in Hillsboro, Aloha, Beaverton, and Scappoose.[^2] Windsor Properties has been in business since 1980 and is a member of the Hillsboro Chamber of Commerce.[^3][^4]

## Key Housing Actions

### As COO of Windsor Properties (present)

- **[SB 1523](/legislation/2026/sb-1523) (2026) — Opposed**: Testified before the Senate Committee on Housing and Development opposing the tenant portal bill, arguing it would limit housing providers' ability to use standard digital tools, require costly parallel systems, increase administrative burden, and ultimately contribute to higher housing costs and reduced housing availability.[^5]

- **[HB 4120](/legislation/2026/hb-4120) (2026) — Supported**: Testified on behalf of Windsor Properties before the House Committee on Housing and Homelessness in support of nonsmoking multifamily housing legislation, arguing that secondhand smoke poses health and safety risks in multifamily housing and that the bill would create consistent protections for market-rate renters similar to those already required in HUD-funded properties.[^6]
