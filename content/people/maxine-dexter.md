# Maxine Dexter

Maxine Dexter served as an Oregon State Representative for District 33 (Northwest Portland) from 2021 to 2024. A physician by training, she was elected to the U.S. House of Representatives in 2024 representing Oregon's 3rd Congressional District.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[HB 3414 (2023)](/legislation/2023/hb-3414)**: Chief sponsor of Governor Kotek's housing production bill (did not pass)
