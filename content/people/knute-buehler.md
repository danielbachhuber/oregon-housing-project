# Knute Buehler

Knute Buehler served as an Oregon State Representative from 2015 to 2019, representing District 54 (Bend). A Republican, he was the party's nominee for Governor in 2018. He was a bipartisan cosponsor of several housing bills during Oregon's early housing reform era.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[HB 4079 (2016)](/legislation/2016/hb-4079)**: Chief sponsor of Affordable Housing Pilot Program
- **[HB 4143 (2016)](/legislation/2016/hb-4143)**: Co-sponsor of tenant notice requirements
- **[HB 2377 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2377)**: Chief co-sponsor of affordable housing tax credit
