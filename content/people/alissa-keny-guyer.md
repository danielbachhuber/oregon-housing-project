# Alissa Keny-Guyer

Alissa Keny-Guyer served as an Oregon State Representative from 2013 to 2021, representing District 46 (Southeast Portland). She was a leading voice on housing affordability and tenant protections during Oregon's housing reform era.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[SB 1533 (2016)](/legislation/2016/sb-1533)**: Key House champion for repealing Oregon's inclusionary zoning ban
- **[HB 4143 (2016)](/legislation/2016/hb-4143)**: Co-sponsor of tenant notice requirements
- **[HB 2007 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2007)**: Co-sponsor of early middle housing bill (did not pass)
- **[HB 2004 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2004)**: Co-sponsor of rent control bill (did not pass)
- **[HB 2002 (2019)](/legislation/2019/hb-2002)**: Sponsor of right of first refusal legislation
- **[HB 2003 (2019)](/legislation/2019/hb-2003)**: Sponsor of Housing Production Strategies
- **[SB 608 (2019)](/legislation/2019/sb-608)**: Sponsor of statewide rent control
- **[HB 4001 (2020)](/legislation/2020/hb-4001)**: Co-sponsor of emergency shelter legislation
