# Sara Gelser

Sara Gelser Blouin is an Oregon State Senator representing District 8 (Corvallis area). She previously served in the Oregon House of Representatives from 2005 to 2014. She has been a key champion of affordable housing and tenant protection legislation.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[SB 1533 (2016)](/legislation/2016/sb-1533)**: Key legislative champion for repealing Oregon's inclusionary zoning ban
- **[HB 2004 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2004)**: Chief sponsor of rent control bill (did not pass)
- **[HB 4123 (2022)](/legislation/2022/hb-4123)**: Co-sponsor of homeless services pilots
