# Pam Marsh

Pam Marsh is an Oregon State Representative serving District 5 (Ashland/Southern Oregon) since 2017. She has been an advocate for affordable housing, particularly manufactured housing as a critical component of housing supply.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[HB 2007 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2007)**: Co-sponsor of early middle housing bill (did not pass)
- **[HB 2377 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2377)**: Chief co-sponsor of affordable housing tax credit
- **[HB 4001 (2020)](/legislation/2020/hb-4001)**: Co-sponsor of emergency shelter legislation
- **[HB 4064 (2022)](/legislation/2022/hb-4064)**: Chief sponsor of manufactured home park protections bill
