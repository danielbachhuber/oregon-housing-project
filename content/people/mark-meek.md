# Mark Meek

Mark Meek is an Oregon State Senator representing District 20 (Clackamas County), previously serving in the Oregon House from 2017 to 2023. He has been an advocate for transit-oriented development and housing production.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[HB 4001 (2020)](/legislation/2020/hb-4001)**: Co-sponsor of emergency shelter legislation
- **[HB 2558 (2021)](/legislation/2021/hb-2558)**: Chief sponsor of transit housing bill (did not pass)
- **[SB 458 (2021)](/legislation/2021/sb-458)**: Co-sponsor of middle housing land division
