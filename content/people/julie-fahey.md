# Julie Fahey

Julie Fahey is an Oregon State Representative serving District 14 (Eugene) since 2017. She has served as Chair of the House Committee on Housing and has been a consistent sponsor and co-sponsor of housing legislation throughout her tenure.

## Key Housing Actions

- **[HB 2007 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2007)**: Co-sponsor of early middle housing bill (did not pass)
- **[HB 2004 (2017)](/legislation/2017/hb-2004)**: Co-sponsor of rent control bill (did not pass)
- **[HB 2001 (2019)](/legislation/2019/hb-2001)**: Sponsor of landmark middle housing legislation
- **[HB 2003 (2019)](/legislation/2019/hb-2003)**: Sponsor of Housing Production Strategies bill
- **[HB 4001 (2020)](/legislation/2020/hb-4001)**: Co-sponsor of emergency shelter legislation
- **[SB 458 (2021)](/legislation/2021/sb-458)**: Co-sponsor of middle housing land division
- **[SB 282 (2021)](/legislation/2021/sb-282)**: House Floor Manager for COVID tenant protections
- **[HB 2558 (2021)](/legislation/2021/hb-2558)**: Co-sponsor of transit housing bill (did not pass)
- **[HB 4051 (2022)](/legislation/2022/hb-4051)**: Requestor of housing omnibus bill
